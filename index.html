Join Us

Vision

To become a leading agency that transforms the entertainment industry by nurturing and advancing Indonesian idols to a global level, creating cultural icons that inspire and entertain millions of fans worldwide.

Mision

  1. To build and develop the best potential of every idol we manage by providing professional training, talent development, and comprehensive mentoring.
  2. To create unique, innovative, and relevant creative content to support the careers and expand the reach of our idols in the national and international markets.
  3. To be a company that delivers positive influence and empowers our idols to act as cultural ambassadors that strengthen Indonesia's image on a global scale.
